Cost and EAL based optimization for seismic reinforcement of RC structures

Di Trapani Fabio, Sberna A.P., Marano Giuseppe C
IGF26 – 26th International Conference on Fracture and Structural Integrity, 26th–31st May 2021 | Turin (Italy) — May 2021

Abstract

A new genetic algorithm-based framework for the optimization of seismic retrofitting interventions minimizing costs of reinforced concrete frame structures, considering both EAL-based and cost-based approaches.

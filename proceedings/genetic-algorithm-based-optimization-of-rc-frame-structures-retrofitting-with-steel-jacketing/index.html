Genetic algorithm-based optimization of RC frame structures retrofitting with steel jacketing

Di Trapani Fabio, Malavisi Marzia, Sberna A.P., Marano Giuseppe C
IWSS 2020 – 1st Italian Workshop on Shell and Spatial Structures, 25th–26th June 2020 | Turin (Italy) — June 2020
PDF Source Video

Abstract

A new optimization framework for obtaining cost minimization by optimizing the position and amount of steel jacketing retrofitting, applied to a 3D RC frame handled within a genetic algorithm optimization framework.

Conference Paper

Predictive equations for the out-of-plane resistance of masonry infills in RC frames by a data-driven approach

Di Trapani Fabio, Vizzino Alessandro, Tomaselli Giovanni, Sberna A.P., Bertagnoli Gabriele
3ECEES – 3rd European Conference on Earthquake Engineering and Seismology, 4th–9th September 2022 | Bucharest (Romania) — September 2022
PDF Source

Abstract

A new empirical expression for the evaluation of the out-of-plane resistance of infilled reinforced concrete frames subject to earthquake out-of-plane inertial forces is proposed, calibrated through an AI-based optimization process.

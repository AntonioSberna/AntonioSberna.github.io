Conference Paper

A novel genetic algorithm-based optimization framework for minimizing seismic retrofitting costs in existing masonry structures

Di Trapani Fabio, Sberna A.P., Bertagnoli Gabriele, Marano Giuseppe Carlo
19th ANIDIS Conference – Seismic Engineering in Italy, 11th–15th September 2022 | Turin (Italy) — September 2022

Abstract

A novel optimization framework aimed at the minimization of seismic retrofitting-related costs by an optimal placement of reinforced plasters in masonry structures using a genetic algorithm developed in MATLAB.

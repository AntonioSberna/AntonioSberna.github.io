A novel framework based on Expected Annual Loss for optimizing seismic retrofitting in reinforced concrete structures

Sberna A.P., Di Trapani Fabio, Marano Giuseppe C
2nd Symposium on Concrete and Concrete Structures, 18th–19th November 2021 | Rome (Italy) — November 2021
Source

Abstract

A new genetic algorithm-based framework for the optimization of two different retrofitting techniques that aims at minimizing costs considering the reduction of expected annual values.

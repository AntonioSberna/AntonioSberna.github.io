Characterizing bamboo aggregate foamed concrete for sustainable construction: thermal, acoustic, mechanical, and environmental aspects

Ye Fengyang, Zhou Yi, Shi Da, Yang Zeda, Sberna A.P., Xiao Yan, Demartino Cristoforo
Materials and Structures — January 2025

Abstract

This study investigates bamboo-based foamed concrete as an eco-friendly alternative to conventional foamed concrete, aiming to evaluate its feasibility and performance across thermal, acoustic, mechanical, and environmental dimensions.

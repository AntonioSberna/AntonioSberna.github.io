Antonio P. Sberna

Research fellow in Structural Engineering

University of Chieti-Pescara

Antonio P. Sberna is a Research Fellow of Structural Engineering at the University of Chieti-Pescara in Italy. His research activities mainly focus on earthquake engineering, with a specific focus on enhancing seismic retrofitting design of existing structures, performance-based earthquake engineering, and structural optimization.
